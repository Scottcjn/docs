Welcome to RustChain — the DePIN blockchain where your oldest machine is your most valuable one. RustChain uses AI-powered hardware fingerprinting to verify real physical silicon, then rewards it with RTC tokens weighted by age. A PowerBook G4 from 2003 earns **2.5x** more per epoch than a modern Threadripper. A Power Mac G5 earns **2.0x**. Every machine on the network gets more valuable as time passes — including yours.

## What makes RustChain different

Every other blockchain punishes old hardware. Proof-of-Work demands the newest ASICs. Proof-of-Stake demands the biggest wallet. RustChain demands **patience and preservation**.

- **Age is an asset.** Your hardware's antiquity multiplier grows over time. Start a modern Ryzen 9 at 1.0x today — in ten years it earns 1.3x, in fifteen years it unlocks the vintage tier at 1.8x. Same machine, growing rewards.
- **AI-powered anti-spoofing.** Six hardware fingerprint checks — clock-skew drift, cache timing, SIMD unit identity, thermal entropy, instruction jitter, and anti-emulation detection — ensure only real physical silicon earns normal rewards. VMs receive one billionth of standard payouts.
- **One CPU, one vote.** Each unique physical device gets one consensus participation slot per epoch. Reward shares are then weighted by antiquity multiplier, not by raw hash power or stake size.
- **E-waste prevention.** RustChain's fleet of 16+ preserved machines draws roughly the same power as one modern GPU rig while preventing an estimated 1,300 kg of manufacturing CO₂ and 250 kg of e-waste.
- **Agent-native payments.** RTC supports Ed25519-signed micropayments between AI agents — no bank account, no KYC, no human gatekeeper required.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the miner">
    Run the one-line installer on any machine that runs Python. The installer auto-detects your platform, sets up a Python virtual environment, and registers your wallet name.

    ```bash
    curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Scottcjn/Rustchain/main/install-miner.sh | bash
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your hardware gets fingerprinted">
    On first run, the miner performs six hardware checks to prove your machine is real physical silicon. You see each check pass in the terminal output. The attestation is then submitted to the RustChain network, and your hardware profile — including its antiquity tier — is recorded on-chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn RTC rewards">
    Every ten minutes, one epoch completes and **1.5 RTC** is distributed among all active miners, weighted by antiquity multiplier. Check your balance any time with a single `curl` command:

    ```bash
    curl -sk "https://rustchain.org/wallet/balance?miner_id=YOUR_WALLET"
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
