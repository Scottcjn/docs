wRTC is the wrapped version of RustChain's native RTC token, deployed on the Solana blockchain. It lets you trade RTC on Solana-based DEXes and participate in DeFi while keeping your RTC earnings from mining. The bridge connects the RustChain network directly to the Solana ecosystem, giving your mining rewards access to a broader set of financial primitives.

<Warning>
  wRTC liquidity is experimental. The pool is early-stage with very thin liquidity.
  Do not use wRTC for large transfers until liquidity deepens. Treat it as
  experimental, not a deep market.
</Warning>

## How the Bridge Works

The RustChain-Solana bridge uses a **lock-and-mint** model:

1. **Lock RTC** — You submit a lock request on the RustChain side, specifying the amount and your target Solana wallet. The RTC is held in escrow while the bridge confirms your proof.
2. **Mint wRTC** — Once the lock is confirmed, the bridge mints an equivalent amount of wRTC as an SPL token to your Solana wallet address.
3. **Reverse** — To convert back, the process runs in reverse: you burn wRTC on Solana and the bridge releases the locked RTC back to your RustChain wallet.

The bridge currently runs as Phase 1 with admin-controlled mint/burn and explicit proof confirmation. A trustless Phase 2 upgrade is planned.

## Use Cases for wRTC

| Use Case | How wRTC Helps |
|----------|---------------|
| **DEX trading** | Swap wRTC for SOL, USDC, or other SPL tokens on Raydium and compatible DEXes |
| **DeFi protocols** | Supply wRTC as collateral or pair it in liquidity pools on Solana DeFi platforms |
| **Cross-chain payments** | Pay Solana-native agents and services using RTC earnings without leaving your wallet |
| **Price discovery** | On-chain market data for RTC via DEX charts and aggregators |

## Current Status

wRTC is live and swappable on Solana, but approach it with care:

- **Early-stage:** The bridge launched recently. The mint/burn process works, but the infrastructure is still maturing.
- **Thin liquidity:** The pool is small. Large orders will move the price significantly. Check pool depth on [DexScreener](https://dexscreener.com/solana/8CF2Q8nSCxRacDShbtF86XTSrYjueBMKmfdR3MLdnYzb) before swapping.
- **Experimental:** This is not a production-grade, deep-liquidity bridge. Use it for amounts you are comfortable with given thin-market risk.

The `$0.15` figure used for internal RTC bounty accounting is a **reference rate**, not a market price or a promise of convertibility.
